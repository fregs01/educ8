Educ8 Online Quiz Platform - Eligibility

Welcome to the Educ8 online quiz platform! To participate, individuals must meet the following eligibility criteria:

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Age: Participants must be at least 18 years old to register for the quiz platform.
  • Education Level: The platform is open to individuals enrolled in educational institutions (high schools, colleges, universities) and lifelong learners seeking knowledge.
  • Registration: Participants must register on the Educ8 platform to access quiz categories.
  • Geographical Restrictions: The platform is available globally unless restricted by local laws or regulations.

Additional Information:

The Educ8 online quiz platform aims to provide an engaging learning experience for participants from various educational backgrounds. Please ensure compliance with the rules and guidelines set forth in the terms of use.

Note: The eligibility criteria are subject to change based on platform policies and guidelines.
