Educ8 Quiz Competition Rules

Below are the rules for the Educ8 quiz competition:

  1. Participant Limit: Each round accommodates up to 100 participants.
  2. Registration and Category Selection:
    • Participants must register for each round through the provided portal.
    • Participants can choose from the following categories:
      • Sports
      • TV Shows
      • English
      • Mathematics
      • General Paper
      • Arts
      • Commercial
    • Multiple attempts per participant are allowed within the registration period for each category.
  3. Competition Structure:
    • Question Format: Questions will be based on the selected category, encompassing various question types.
    • Round Duration: Each round will consist of a predetermined set of questions.
    • Time Limit: Participants must complete the quiz within a specified time frame.
  4. Participant Attempts:
    • Participants can make multiple attempts within the registration period for each category.
    • Only the highest score achieved by a participant in a specific category will be considered for qualification and ranking.
  5. Lifelines:
    • Participants can earn lifelines by achieving specific milestones or high scores in each category.
    • Additional lifelines can be purchased by participants through designated methods (e.g., points, virtual currency).
  6. Selection Process:
    • Points are awarded for each correct answer; rankings are based on total scores within the chosen category.
    • A live or updated leaderboard displays participant rankings throughout the round within each category, including split-second completion times.
    • In case of a tie in rank, the participant who completed the quiz in the shortest time, down to split-seconds, will be ranked higher. This timing is displayed on the live rank table.
  7. Round Management:
    • A round ends once 100 participants have completed the quiz within each category or when the registration period expires, whichever comes first.
    • Winners or qualifiers for the next round within each category are announced after each completed round, considering both score and completion time in case of ties.
  8. Prizes and Recognition:
    • Prizes or recognition are awarded to top performers of each round within each category.
    • Qualifiers from each category compete in a final round for grand prizes or special recognition.
  9. Technical Requirements:
    • Participants should ensure a stable internet connection during the competition.
    • The quiz platform should be compatible with common browsers and devices.
  10. Disqualification: Participants found violating the rules may face disqualification from the competition.
  11. Feedback and Support: Participants can provide feedback or seek support related to the quiz competition through designated channels.
  12. Admin Authority and Features: The administration reserves the right to add more features based on suggestions from participants or as deemed suitable for the competition's improvement.
  13. Audience Engagement: Where feasible, audience interaction or engagement might be included in the competition structure.
